Welcome to Say it with Sweets

Cookies, cakes, cupcakes, and more!

"Sweet" gifts for everyone or for your special celebration. We have you covered. We offer:

  • Cakes
  • Cupcakes
  • Cookies
  • Cookie Trays
  • Cupcake Bouquets
  • and more!

Custom made cookies and cupcakes must be ordered at least 7 working days in advance. Regular and gourmet cupcakes at least 1-2 working days in advance. Cookie cakes and cookie trays - at least 5 working days in advance.

Please note: Order as early as possible. During very busy weeks we may not be able to accommodate you.

NOTICE: We don’t accept walk-in orders. All orders must be submitted online or through phone.
